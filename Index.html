ABOUT US

The newly established company Inveishjon focuses the worlds hunger problems. We want to stop the worlds hunger problems. To reach our goal we want to focus on getting food from the oceans. That way we do not use our valuable land space and we get food.

Products

Inveishjon wants to build many of these breeders, but bigger and out in the ocean. That is so that we do not use our valuable land space. In these breeders it will be possible to feed thousands of people after some donations.

We also want to make robot cleaners that goes underwater and cleans the oceans for us. These can cover big areals and clean up plastic and other trash that gets dumped into the oceans. These robots will insure that the oceans stay clean.
